Deploying a Compliant CRM Onboarding Platform Across 70+ Banks: From Chaos to Standardization

Company M2P Fintech
Role Project Manager
Duration May 2024 – May 2025
Geography Gujarat & South Maharashtra
70+Banks Deployed
25%Faster Delivery
30%Manual Effort Saved
100%RBI Compliance

The Challenge

M2P Fintech's mandate was ambitious: implement a unified CRM and Core Banking System (CBS) onboarding platform across 70+ banks in the Gujarati and South Maharashtra regions — standardizing account opening, term deposits, loan creation, cash credit, and overdraft management, while maintaining full compliance with RBI, KYC, and AML regulations.

The complexity was significant: each bank had unique operational requirements, legacy CBS systems, and their own interpretation of RBI compliance guidelines. There was no single template that fit all — and attempting to force one would have caused failures across the entire deployment portfolio.

My Role

Project Manager — leading cross-functional Agile teams through requirements definition, CBS integration design, AML module implementation, UAT governance, and go-live execution across all 70+ bank deployments simultaneously.

The Approach

Agile Governance at Scale

  • Implemented Scrum and Kanban across parallel workstreams, running sprint ceremonies (planning, stand-ups, reviews, retrospectives) for multiple delivery tracks simultaneously without letting any fall behind.
  • Used JIRA/Confluence as the single source of truth for backlog, progress, and compliance traceability — enabling MIS teams to generate real-time reports without manual effort.
  • Built automated dashboards that reduced manual reporting effort by 30%, giving stakeholders instant visibility into delivery health across all 70+ deployments.
  • Tracked KPIs continuously and presented outcomes to senior leadership, maintaining full transparency on delivery and financial performance.

Compliance-by-Design Architecture

  • Embedded KYC refresh workflows, AML screening, and FRM (Financial Risk Management) modules into the core onboarding journey — built into the product architecture, not added post-launch.
  • Worked directly with bank compliance officers, MIS teams, and RBI-aligned stakeholders to validate every workflow against regulatory requirements before development began.
  • Achieved full compliance across all 70+ bank deployments, with zero major regulatory findings post-launch — a clean record across the entire portfolio.
  • Ensured periodic KYC refresh and screening were embedded as continuous compliance mechanisms, not one-time onboarding checks.

CBS Integration & UI/UX Delivery

  • Oversaw design and integration of a role-based, intuitive UI supporting accounts, FD/RD term deposits, loans, and cash credit products — built on top of each bank's existing CBS infrastructure without requiring core system replacement.
  • Managed UAT rigorously: GUI flow validation, functional testing, edge case scenarios, and user acceptance sign-off were mandatory before any go-live authorization.
  • Ensured the integrated AML and FRM modules effectively detected and flagged risks, strengthening the overall security posture across all deployed banks.

"Standardization across 70+ banks sounds straightforward until you realize every bank has its own interpretation of RBI guidelines. Success came from building compliance INTO the product architecture — not bolting it on later."

Impact

  • Delivered the CRM onboarding platform across 70+ banks, improving customer onboarding efficiency and compliance consistency across the entire regional portfolio.
  • Reduced project turnaround time by 25% through Agile optimization and parallel workstream management across multiple simultaneous deployments.
  • Automated dashboards and reporting reduced manual effort by 30%, enabling data-driven decision-making at the MIS level without additional headcount.
  • Full regulatory compliance achieved across all deployments — RBI, KYC, AML — with no major audit findings across the entire 70+ bank portfolio.
  • Enhanced MIS capabilities through integrated reporting modules, enabling banks to generate accurate, timely data for internal governance and regulatory reporting.

Why This Matters

  • Deploying a compliant banking platform at this geographic scale is a coordination problem as much as a technology problem — the PM function was the connective tissue that made it work.
  • Compliance-by-design is the only sustainable model in regulated industries — retrofitting compliance after the product ships creates technical debt, regulatory exposure, and customer trust erosion simultaneously.
  • Automated governance at the MIS level transforms how banks make decisions — moving from reactive to proactive risk management.
